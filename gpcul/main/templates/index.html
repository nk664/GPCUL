About the System

Built for Students,
Designed for Staff

GPCUL – Govt. Polytechnic College Udhampur Library System is a comprehensive digital platform developed to modernize library operations at our institution. It bridges the gap between traditional library management and modern technology, making it easier for students to discover resources and for library staff to manage daily operations efficiently.

Whether it's issuing books, tracking returns, managing fines, or generating reports — GPCUL handles it all from a single, intuitive dashboard.

  • Streamlined book issue & return workflows
  • Automated due-date reminders and fine calculations
  • Real-time inventory tracking and availability status
  • Secure admin panel with role-based access control
